Privacy Policy

Dr. Ashraf Saiyed — Instagram Automation Tools

Overview

This application connects to Instagram Business/Creator accounts through Meta's official Instagram Graph API to help the account owner manage their own content — publishing posts, reading analytics, managing comments, and responding to direct messages. It is operated by Dr. Ashraf Saiyed for personal and authorized use.

What Data We Access

With the explicit authorization of the account owner, the app may access:

  • Basic account information (username, follower count, media count, bio)
  • The account's own posts, reels, and their performance insights (reach, views, likes, comments)
  • Comments on the account's own posts
  • Direct messages sent to the account, in order to respond to them

How We Use It

Data is used solely to perform actions the account owner explicitly requests — such as publishing a post, reading analytics, replying to a comment, or answering a direct message. The app does not use the data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the requested action.

Data Storage & Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party.
  • The app does not maintain its own database of your Instagram content. Data is fetched from Instagram only at the moment an action is performed and is not retained afterward.
  • Access tokens are stored securely as server environment variables and are never exposed publicly or committed to source control.

Data Retention & Deletion

Because the app does not store your Instagram data, there is nothing to retain or delete on our side. To revoke the app's access entirely, the account owner can remove the app's permissions at any time via Instagram → Settings → Apps and Websites, or from the Meta Developer console.

Third-Party Services

The app relies on Meta's Instagram Graph API. Your use of Instagram is governed by Instagram's own Privacy Policy and Meta's Terms.
